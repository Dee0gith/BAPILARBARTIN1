Durano Eco Farm & Spring Resort

Carmen, Cebu

A nature retreat in Carmen, Cebu, this eco-resort features refreshing spring pools surrounded by tropical gardens and forests. Visitors can enjoy natural spring water, picnic areas, and farm activities, making it a great spot for family outings and relaxation. The resort’s rustic and eco-friendly atmosphere provides a refreshing escape from the city’s hustle and bustle.
