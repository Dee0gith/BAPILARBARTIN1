Cebu Ocean Park

South Road Properties, Cebu

The largest oceanarium in the Philippines, Cebu Ocean Park features a variety of marine and freshwater species, interactive exhibits, and an immersive underwater tunnel. Visitors can experience close encounters with stingrays, sharks, and colorful tropical fish. The park also offers exciting attractions like fish spa therapy and the chance to walk underwater with marine animals through its Sea Trek activity.
