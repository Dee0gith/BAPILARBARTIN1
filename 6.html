Canyoneering/Kawasan Falls

Badian, Cebu

One of Cebu’s top adventure activities, canyoneering in Badian involves trekking, jumping off waterfalls, and swimming through turquoise waters leading to the famous Kawasan Falls. Kawasan Falls itself is a multi-tiered waterfall known for its stunning blue waters and lush surroundings. This thrilling experience is perfect for adventure seekers who want to explore Cebu’s natural beauty in an exciting way.
