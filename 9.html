Simala Shrine

Sibonga, Cebu

Also known as the Monastery of the Holy Eucharist, Simala Shrine is a grand and sacred pilgrimage site in Cebu. The shrine, built like a castle, is a popular destination for devotees praying for miracles and blessings from the Virgin Mary. It features stunning architecture, religious relics, and a peaceful ambiance that attracts both tourists and worshippers.
