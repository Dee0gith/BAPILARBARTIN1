Bantayan Island

Bantayan, Cebu

Located off the northern coast of Cebu, Bantayan Island is famous for its powdery white sand beaches, crystal-clear waters, and laid-back island vibe. It is a perfect getaway for those looking to relax and enjoy a peaceful tropical paradise. Visitors can explore the island by bike, visit historical sites, or go island hopping to nearby sandbars and secluded beaches.
